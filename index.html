About PCX productions

Ah yes, the dreaded midlife crisis—the moment when your brain, after years of pretending to be a responsible adult, suddenly decides it wants a refund. Should I abandon everything and become a goose farmer, spending my days honking at the sky in mutual frustration with my feathered comrades? Or perhaps I should take up blacksmithing, forging swords like some medieval artisan, only to realize I have neither the patience nor the upper body strength? Maybe I'll try gardening, nurturing delicate little plants until they inevitably perish under my neglect. Or, and hear me out, I could just embrace the chaos, buy a drone, and film some breathtaking aerial shots — at least then I'd be capturing something beautiful while figuring things out.
